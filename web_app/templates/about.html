What is this EmoContext?

EmoContext aims to be a smart system that predicts emotions based on a conversation between two people. Considering the fact that emotion detection is one of the most complex field of study when it comes to NLP, this system aims to detect emotions part of only four classes: happy, sad, angry and others.

The system is based on an artificial neural network with an unique and complex structure. The neural network receives as an input a conversation made of 3 sentences and it predicts, based on these sentences, the general emotion of the conversation with an accuracy of almost 90%.
